Rubric

Clear, fair criteria used by industry professionals.

Judging

Evaluated by business professionals from relevant industries.

Identifying the Problem (20 pt)

How pertinent and clearly framed is the problem?

The Solution (30 pt)

How feasible, logical, and impactful is your project?

Creativity / Innovation (20 pt)

How original, bold, and inventive is your approach?

Fits Theme (20 pt)

How well does your project align with the STEAM division you selected?

Presentation Quality (10 pt)

How professional, engaging, and well-structured is your presentation?

Additional Notes

* Art is graded primarily on Creativity and Presentation.

* Projects placing highly across 3 divisions can win the best prizes!
