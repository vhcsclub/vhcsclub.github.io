Rubric

Judging: Done by business professionals from the respective industry.

  • Identifying the problem (20pt): How pertinent is your problem?
  • The solution (30pt): How feasible and logical is your project?
  • Creativity / Innovation (20pt): How innovative and DARING is your project?
  • Fits theme (20pt): How well does your project fit the theme? (TBD)
  • Presentation Quality (10pt): How professional and engaging is it?
