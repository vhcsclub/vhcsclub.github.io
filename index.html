What we’re about

VHCS empowers students to explore coding, problem-solving, and technology through hands-on activities, competitions, and community projects. No experience required — just curiosity.

Programs & Opportunities

Pick a path or try them all — each leads to real, showcase-ready work.

Courses & Prep

AP/dual-credit guidance, study groups, and curated resources to help you thrive in CS classes.

XP Coding & Consulting

Workshop series and client-style projects that turn your skills into portfolio pieces.

Girls Who Code @ VHHS

Inclusive space focused on representation and belonging in tech.

Web & App Dev

Design and build modern websites or apps, learn frontend/back-end and deployment basics.

Workshops

Short, practical sessions — Git, HTML/CSS/JS, Python, APIs, data, AI tools, and more.

D128 STEAM Showcase

Submit your project, present to judges, and earn recognition across STEAM divisions.
